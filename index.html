ABOUT ME

My name is Russell, but you can call me Russ. I currently live in the Sacramento, CA area and am a Northern California native. All throughout my life, I've loved the creative aspect of life; so at a very young age I began to play music and try my hand at a bit of drawing/sketching.

That creative interest, over years developed into my getting involved in graphic design. At first, just for fun, and then professionally as a freelancer. I've done many things professionally in my short adult life, but mostly I have been employed in the optical field/industry. I was a licensed optician and worked several years doing that until I began teaching it at a vocational college.

An opportunity opened for me to pursue my more creative interests and train as a software developer with Thinkful. So I jumped at the chance. Ever since I've loved combining my graphic design skills and web development skills to be apart some great projects. Some of which I hope you'll checkout below.
